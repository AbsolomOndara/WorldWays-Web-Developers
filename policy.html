1. Introduction

At WorldWays Web-Developers, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, business details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Communicate with you (e.g., project updates, invoices).
  • Enhance website security and user experience.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transfers.
  • Secure servers with restricted access.
  • Regular security audits.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., PayPal, Stripe).
  • Hosting providers (for website deployment).
  • Legal authorities (if required by law).

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Improve site functionality.
  • Analyze traffic via Google Analytics.
  • Remember user preferences.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Kenyan data laws, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page periodically for changes.
